2. Understanding Laser Hair Removal

2.1 What is Laser Hair Removal?

Laser hair removal is a medical procedure that uses concentrated beams of light (lasers) to remove unwanted hair. The process targets the pigment in hair follicles, effectively damaging them and inhibiting future hair growth.

2.2 Benefits of Laser Hair Removal

  • Long-lasting results: Unlike traditional methods like shaving and waxing, laser hair removal offers permanent reduction in hair growth.
  • Precision: Lasers can selectively target dark, coarse hairs while leaving the surrounding skin undamaged.
  • Speed: Each laser pulse takes a fraction of a second and can treat multiple hairs at once. Small areas, such as the upper lip, can be treated in under a minute.
